PayPal JavaScript payment buttons

Integrating with PayPal's payment buttons are as easy as including a small snippet of code. We have multiple flavors of buttons to fit your needs:

  • Buy Now
  • Add to Cart
  • Hosted
  • QR Codes

Buy Now buttons are perfect for single item purchases Customize

<script src="paypal-button.min.js?merchant=6XF3MPZBZV6HU"
    data-button="buynow"
    data-name="My product"
    data-amount="1.00"
></script>
Email address or merchant ID

Add To Cart buttons lets users add multiple items instantly giving your website a cart Customize

<script src="paypal-button-minicart.min.js?merchant=6XF3MPZBZV6HU"
    data-button="cart"
    data-name="Product in your cart"
    data-amount="1.00"
></script>
Email address or merchant ID

Hosted buttons are extra secure and provide protection to your product data Customize

<script src="paypal-button.min.js?merchant=6XF3MPZBZV6HU"
    data-button="cart"
    data-id="8R63A645E2QB6"
></script>
Email address or merchant ID

<script src="paypal-button.min.js?merchant=6XF3MPZBZV6HU"
